2. How to use?

需要插件

  • Windows Media Player Plug-in，在chrome://plugins可以找到

设置插件权限，两种方法

  • chrome里访问此地址chrome://plugins，找到Windows Media Player Plug-in，选上Always allowed
  • Settings->Show advanced settings->Privacy->Content settings->Plug-ins->Run automatically, 然后Manage exceptions，添加允许chrome-extension://
